## 🚀 WHAT'S NEW

You can now mark leads as **"Lost"** or **"On Hold"** (or any status) with **just 1 click** directly from the **All Leads** table!

### ✨ How It Works:

In the **"All Leads"** view:
1. The **Status column** now shows a **dropdown** instead of just a badge
2. **Click the dropdown** → See all 8 statuses:
   - New
   - Contacted
   - Qualified
   - Proposal Sent
   - Negotiation
   - ✅ Won
   - ❌ Lost
   - ⏸️ On Hold

3. **Select any status** → Instant update!

---

## 📋 3 WAYS TO CHANGE STATUS

### **Method 1: Quick Change (NEW!)** ⚡
**Location**: All Leads Table  
**How**: Click status dropdown → Select "Lost" or "On Hold"  
**Best For**: Quick status updates

### **Method 2: Lead Detail Modal**
**Location**: Any lead card → Click to open details  
**How**: Edit status in detail view  
**Best For**: Full lead review + status change

### **Method 3: Bulk Action**
**Location**: All Leads Table  
**How**: 
1. Select multiple leads (checkboxes)
2. Click "Change Status" in bulk action bar
3. Choose status
**Best For**: Updating many leads at once
