Do you handle both the digital marketing services AND the software?
+
Yes. Subix is a service + product company. Our team manages your website, ad campaigns, and SEO. Our software (LeadOS + HRMS) runs your team's day-to-day operations. You get both under one relationship — not two vendors.
What does the hospital website include?
+
Your website includes a homepage, services/specialities pages, doctor profiles, an online appointment request form, and a mobile-first responsive design. It's built for SEO so patients searching "hospital in [city]" or "[speciality] near me" can find you.
How do patient inquiries reach LeadOS?

Any inquiry from your website form, WhatsApp number, or Google My Business goes directly into LeadOS. Your coordinators see all inquiries in one dashboard and can assign follow-ups immediately.

Yes. HRMS supports multiple employee roles, departments, and shift types. Each department sees only its own staff. The admin gets a full hospital-wide view.
