The Problem

Why Real Estate Teams Bleed Revenue

These three problems cost Indian property businesses crores every year. All three are fixable.

Problem 01

5 Stores, 5 Excel Files, Zero Unified View

Each store manager tracks attendance separately. Leave requests come via WhatsApp. Payroll is assembled from 5 different sheets at month end. One error cascades into 3 correction emails.

Result: 3–4 days of HR work every month, errors, delays
Problem 02

Franchise Enquiries Going Cold in WhatsApp Chats

You get 15 franchise enquiries a month from your website and LinkedIn. They go to a WhatsApp number, get a manual reply, and then nobody follows up systematically. 10 of them choose a competitor's franchise.

Result: Franchise expansion stalled by poor follow-up

No Brand Visibility Online — Customers Can't Find Your Stores

Your stores are good. But customers searching for your product category on Google find a competitor first because they have a better website and active Google listings.

Result: Footfall going to better-ranked competitors

Do you handle both the digital marketing services AND the software?
+
Yes. Subix is a service + product company. Our team manages your website, ad campaigns, and SEO. Our software (LeadOS + HRMS) runs your team's day-to-day operations. You get both under one relationship — not two vendors.
Can different store managers see only their own staff and not other stores?
+
Yes. In HRMS you can set location-based access — each store manager sees only their team. The brand admin sees all stores from the top-level dashboard.

Yes. You can set custom pipeline stages for franchise onboarding — Enquiry, Intro Call, Site Visit, LOI Signed, Agreement, Store Launch. Move investors through the funnel and follow up at each stage.
