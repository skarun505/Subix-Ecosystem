The Problem

Why Real Estate Teams Bleed Revenue

These three problems cost Indian property businesses crores every year. All three are fixable.

Problem 01

Admission Season = 500 Inquiries, Zero System

Every April and October your team is flooded with calls, WhatsApps, and website forms. Counsellors manage it all from memory and sticky notes. Half the inquiries never get a second call.

Result: 40–50% of interested students enrol elsewhere
Problem 02

Parents Follow Up 3 Times and Still Hear Nothing

A parent inquires about your engineering coaching batch on a Saturday. Counsellor sees it Monday afternoon. By then the parent has already visited a competitor and paid the registration fee.

Result: High-intent admissions lost to response delay
Problem 03

Faculty Payroll Takes 3 Days Every Month

Part-time faculty, visiting lecturers, admin staff, housekeeping — all calculated separately in Excel. One mistake causes 5 correction emails. Payroll runs 3–4 days late.

Result: Staff dissatisfaction, compliance risk, management time wasted

+

Can LeadOS track inquiries across multiple branches or centres?
+
Yes. You can set up multiple locations in LeadOS, assign counsellors branch-wise, and get a combined view across all centres from one admin login.

+
Yes. You can add custom fields in LeadOS like course, batch, and class level. Filter and sort inquiries by any of these fields at any time.
